Kamala Sohonie

Breaking barriers for scientific Indian women

Timeline in Dr. Sohonie's life

  • 1911 - Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh
  • 1933 - Graduates at the top of her class in chemistry and physics
  • 1936 - Publishes her thesis and obtains her master's degree
  • 1937 - Earns a research scholarship at Cambridge University
  • 1838 - Finishes her thesis and obtains her PhD
  • 1939 - Returns to India, appointed Professor and Head of the Department of Biochemistry at Lady Hardinge Medical College in New Delhi.
  • 1947 - Marries M.V Sohonie and moves to Mumbai, where she joins the Royal Institute of Science as a Professor in the Department of Biochemistry
  • 1982-1983 - Elected President of the Consumer Guidance Society of India
  • 1998 - Dies, after collapsing during a felicitation ceremony organised by Indian Council of Medical Research in New Delhi.
  • 2023 - search engine Google commemorated Sohonie with a Doodle on her 112th birth anniversary.
